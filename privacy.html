Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Legion Hunters collects, uses, and protects information when you play the game, create an account, or use online features.

Information We Collect

  • Account identifiers, display names, and email addresses if provided.
  • Epic Games account information if you sign in with Epic Games, such as Epic account ID, display name, language, and linked account display names permitted by Epic Account Services.
  • Game progress, resources, units, quests, rankings, guild/social data, arena records, settings, and gameplay state.
  • Technical information such as timestamps, session identifiers, diagnostics, and security logs.

How We Use Information

  • To create, authenticate, and manage player accounts.
  • To save and restore game progress.
  • To operate online systems such as rankings, guilds, quests, social features, and account support tools.
  • To detect abuse, cheating, fraud, or unauthorized access.
  • To troubleshoot bugs, improve stability, and respond to support requests.

Third-Party Services

Legion Hunters may use third-party services to provide authentication, database hosting, online persistence, payments, analytics, or platform features. These may include Supabase for account/game data infrastructure and Epic Games for Epic Account Services login.

Epic Games Login

If you choose to sign in with Epic Games, Epic may show a consent screen describing the information shared with Legion Hunters. We use that information only for account login, account linking, player identification, and game service operation.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help operate the game, comply with legal obligations, protect the game and players, or process requests you make.

Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as needed to provide the game, maintain records, resolve disputes, prevent abuse, or comply with legal obligations.

Your Choices

You may choose not to connect an Epic Games account. You may request account deletion or privacy support using the contact method below.
